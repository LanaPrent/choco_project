What topic is best when learning how to make a website?

A serious one about web development? Or perhaps something more relaxing, like a hobby?

If it is serious, it might make things harder and potentially overwhelming. If it is about a hobby, it could be too tempting to focus on the subject rather than the learning material... It definitely has to be something interesting enough to hold my attention but not distracting — something refreshing, perhaps sweet, but not too sweet... invigorating my mind while not weakening my body...

And then, there was that wonderful classic film Chocolat with Juliette Binoche, Judi Dench, Johnny Depp, and Alfred Molina.

And voilà! A dark chocolate topic was born.

It turned out that this topic was actually not just sweet but also well worth exploring. Just like pretty much anything in life, it seems, sweet is never far from bitter, pleasure is never far from peril. As it turns out, it always boils down to balancing invigorating and weakening decisions in life.

To sum up, this website can be thought of as providing some very useful information about dark chocolate consumption — its many benefits and possible downsides. On another level, it might even be understood as a suggestion for how to navigate a tempting learning process that often demands a lot of time and patience, and snacks.
